TarAttendance Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 5, 2025

Thank you for using TarAttendance. Protecting your privacy is extremely important to us. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal information. Please read this policy carefully to understand how we handle your data.

Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Personal Identification Information: Including but not limited to your name, username, and password.
  • Device Information: We collect your device model, device ID, and other device-related information to ensure your account security and provide a better service experience.
  • Login and Usage Data: Including your login time, usage duration, and in-app behavior information.
  • Location Information: We may collect your IP address, but we do not track your precise geographic location.

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for:

  • Providing, maintaining, and improving our services
  • Processing and recording your attendance records
  • Verifying your identity and device
  • Processing your requests and feedback
  • Preventing fraud and abusive behavior
  • Sending service-related notifications
  • Complying with legal requirements or protecting our legitimate interests

Data Storage and Security

We use Firebase as our backend service, and your data is stored on Google Firebase servers. We take the following measures to protect your data:

  • All data transmission processes use SSL/TLS encryption
  • Implementation of access control measures to ensure only authorized personnel can access your data
  • Regular review and updates to our security measures
  • Encryption of sensitive information such as passwords

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. However, we may share your information in the following circumstances:

  • Based on your explicit consent
  • With our service providers, such as Firebase (Google)
  • To comply with legal obligations, court orders, or government requests
  • To protect the rights, property, or safety of TarAttendance, our users, or the public

Advertising

Our application uses Google AdMob to provide advertising services. AdMob may use device identifiers and other information to provide personalized ads. You can manage your advertising preferences through your device settings or by visiting Google Ad Settings.

Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed at children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us, and we will take steps to delete this information.

Your Rights

According to applicable data protection laws, you may have the following rights:

  • Access to your personal data
  • Correction of inaccurate personal data
  • Deletion of your personal data
  • Restriction of or objection to the processing of your personal data
  • Data portability rights
  • Withdrawal of your consent

To exercise these rights, please contact us using the contact information below.

Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page, and we will notify you of significant changes via in-app notifications or email. We encourage you to review this privacy policy periodically to stay informed about how we protect your information.

Cookies and Similar Technologies

Our application and website may use cookies and similar technologies to improve user experience and collect usage statistics. These technologies may collect information such as your device type, operating system, browser type, and other anonymous statistical data.

Third-Party Links

Our application may contain links to third-party websites or services. Please note that the privacy policies of these websites are not related to our policy. We recommend that you read their privacy policies when visiting these websites or using these services.
